The Story Behind Maya Events

At Maya Events, we are a passionate team of event planners, designers, and coordinators dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences. From concept to execution, we bring life to your celebrations with creativity and perfection.

What We Do

Maya Adfinity Corporate Communication Pvt. Ltd. is a dynamic company catering to diverse needs in the media and entertainment sectors. With an ever-expanding clientele, we have strategically diversified into multiple verticals, including Events, Digital Marketing, Digital Communications, Printing, Branding, Promotional Gateways, and more. At Maya Adfinity, we believe in delivering services that are not only creative but also strategically impactful. Our expertise allows us to craft tailor-made solutions that empower clients across industries to achieve their goals effectively. Driven by 18+ years of experience and a relentless commitment to excellence, we continue to be a trusted partner in driving the success of our clients' businesses.

Where We Do

At Maya Adfinity, we provide complete end-to-end event planning services that cover every stage of your event, From concept and design to planning and execution, we ensure each detail is handled with care, delivering outstanding experiences that are both cost efficient and highly satisfying. Our expertise includes exhibitions, festivals, conferences, corporate launches, government programs, as well as live and virtual events that have consistently exceeded expectations and engaged audiences. What sets us apart is the quality of our event support services, including technical solutions, crowd management, guest handling, and foreign delegation reception. With a team of skilled professionals and by following globally accepted standards, we continue to maintain our strong reputation as one of the leading names in the events industry.
